Everything You Need

Built for real healing, not distraction.

📖

Daily Reading + Reflection

Each day combines evidence-based reading with guided journaling — not just advice, but structured CBT exercises.

📊

Mood Tracking

Record your emotional state daily. Watch your trend line improve over 30 days with an interactive line chart.

🏆

30 Badges

One unique badge per day — from "Awakener" to "Phoenix Rising". Small wins keep you going.

🌐

Bilingual

Full Chinese (zh) and English (en) support. Switch anytime without losing progress.

🎵

Ambient Music

3 generative soundtracks via Web Audio API — piano chords, deep space pad, and gentle rain. No downloads needed.

📱

Android Widget

Native homescreen widget showing your daily task, progress bar, and affirmation. Refreshes every 30 minutes.

🔒

100% Private

Your journal and mood data stay on your own machine. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

🆓

Free & Open Source

MIT License. Self-host on any machine with Python. Or deploy via Cloudflare Tunnel for mobile access.
